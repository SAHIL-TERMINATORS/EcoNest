Reduce Plastic Waste

Start by cutting down single-use plastics in your daily life. Use cloth bags, glass bottles, and biodegradable alternatives whenever possible.

Recycle & Reuse

Encourage recycling and upcycling to minimize environmental impact. Separate your waste correctly and support local recycling initiatives.

Spread Awareness

Educate others about the harmful effects of plastic pollution. Participate in campaigns, social media drives, and community clean-up events.
