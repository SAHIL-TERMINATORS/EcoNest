Why is Plastic Pollution a Problem?

Plastic waste is choking our oceans, harming wildlife, and polluting our environment. Every year, millions of tons of plastic end up in landfills and oceans, taking centuries to decompose while releasing harmful chemicals.

The average person consumes about 5 grams of plastic each week through food and water - equivalent to a credit card's worth of microplastics entering our bodies.

Our Mission

To eliminate plastic pollution by promoting sustainable alternatives and educating communities across India about responsible consumption.

Impact Statistics

India generates 9.46 million tons of plastic waste annually. Only 60% gets recycled, leaving massive amounts polluting our environment.

Government Action

India has banned specific single-use plastics. Many states have implemented extended producer responsibility policies for plastic waste.

Sustainable Alternatives

Transitioning away from plastic is easier than you think. Here are practical alternatives:

  • Carry reusable cloth bags for shopping
  • Use stainless steel or bamboo straws
  • Choose glass or metal containers for storage
  • Opt for compostable packaging materials
  • Support brands using biodegradable materials
