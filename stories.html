Kannur: India's First Plastic-Free District

Kannur district in Kerala successfully eliminated single-use plastics, becoming India's first plastic-free district in April 2017. The initiative involved phasing out single-use plastics, prohibiting their sale and purchase, and implementing strict monitoring of waste disposal.
